About

Welcome to Connect To Care's online volunteer platform. This application will allow you to easily register for Connect To Care powered events and will provide you with notifications.

To get started, sign in using your Google account and register for events. Be aware that some events may require special qualification and are not offered to all.

 Our Mission

Connect To Care aims to provide everyone a chance to be the change in their lives and empower themselves to carve their own future.

As part of this, we strive to connect student volunteers to organizations that need them the most. Contact us to learn more about using our platform for your organization.
